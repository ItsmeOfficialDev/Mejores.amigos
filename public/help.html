HELP & RULES

🎮 HOW TO JOIN

Enter your name and join the lobby. Admin accounts are accessed by appending "admin" to the username (e.g., "ishanadmin") and entering the group password.

💰 BIDDING MECHANICS

Starting bid for every player is ₹5 Lakh. Bids must be placed in increments of ₹5 Lakh. Use the "CUSTOM BID" button to leap ahead of the competition!

🛡️ TEAM SQUAD LIMITS

Every manager must complete an 18-player squad within the ₹15 Crore budget:

  • 3 Goalkeepers (GK)
  • 5 Defenders (DEF)
  • 5 Midfielders (MID)
  • 5 Forwards (FWD)

🛑 RESERVE MONEY RULE

Strategic budget management is key. You must always maintain a reserve of ₹10 Lakh for every remaining empty slot in your squad to ensure you can complete your team.
